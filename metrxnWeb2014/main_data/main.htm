Welcome to MetRxn

MetRxn is a comprehensive collection of consistent metabolite and reaction entities for use in metabolic analysis and model construction. MetRxn's current 62,345 unique metabolites and 56142 unique reactions incorporates data from 6 different metabolic databases and 32 genome-scale metabolic models.
